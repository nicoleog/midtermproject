Our Mission

Evergreen Apparel is committed to lasting social and environmental change. Every step we take in our production process supports farms and factories with fair wages, safe facilities, and sustainable materials.




We're on a mission to plant millions of trees and make a positive impact on the world. For every order you place, we plant a tree. We partner with organizations that plant trees to naturally replenish the environment, while at the same time providing food, shelter, and economy to communities around the world.
